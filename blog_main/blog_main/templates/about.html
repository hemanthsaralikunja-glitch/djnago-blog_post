About Pulse Blog

Pulse Blog is a dynamic blog project designed to showcase articles, categories, and author stories with modern page navigation.

We created a richer experience with dedicated pages for featured stories, category collections, and contact outreach so your site feels like a real editorial platform.

What makes this project unique?

  • Featured story highlights
  • Dedicated category landing pages
  • Professional responsive layout
  • Contact form and user-friendly site structure

Editorial vision

Our goal is to provide clean presentation for every article, with easy browsing across topics, strong visuals, and a simple navigation flow.

Every page is built to feel polished and distinct while still being easy to maintain inside Django.

Site features

  • Blog detail pages with related post recommendations.
  • Category list pages with dynamic content.
  • Admin-managed blog posts and categories.
  • Responsive Bootstrap design.
